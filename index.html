Niagara Falls

Dream Destination

Welcome to Niagara Falls, a breathtaking natural wonder that draws millions of visitors annually.

With its towering waterfalls, majestic cliffs, and stunning scenery, this must-see destination promises to leave you in awe. From thrilling boat rides to scenic hikes, the area offers a range of activities for all kinds of travelers. Come and experience the wonder of this incredible destination for yourself and create memories to last a lifetime.

TOP ATTRACTIONS

NIAGARA CITY CRUISE

Experience the thrill of the mist aboard Canada's top visitor attraction - the Voyage to the Falls boat tour. Departing from Niagara Falls, Canada, this 20-minute tour takes you on a journey of a lifetime. Marvel at the stunning views of the Niagara Gorge, American Falls, and Bridal Veil Falls, and get up close and personal with the famous Canadian Horseshoe Falls. Feel the power and hear the roar of the falls while surrounded by the refreshing mist (provided with a souvenir recyclable mist poncho). For a unique experience, take the tour after sunset to witness the illumination of the falls and enjoy a light mist experience.

ZIPLINE TO THE FALLS

Embark on a thrilling adventure with WildPlay's Zipline to the Falls at Grand View Marketplace. Soar down 670 meters towards the base of the famous Canadian Horseshoe Falls and glide past the majestic American Falls. This fully-guided, hands-free experience is suitable for both thrill seekers and hesitant adventurers alike. Witness the awe-inspiring beauty of Niagara Falls from a bird's eye view and land atop the falls observation deck. This once-in-a-lifetime opportunity is available to guests over the age of seven. Don't miss out on the chance to feel the thrill of adventure and witness the beauty of Niagara Falls like never before.

THE TUNNEL AT THE POWER STATION

Explore the fascinating history of the century-old power station from a truly unique perspective, 55 meters beneath the main generating floor. Descend into the tunnel in a glass-paneled elevator and marvel at the power station's hidden decks, which were excavated in 1901 using rudimentary tools such as pickaxes, shovels, and lanterns. Follow the path of the water and journey through the tunnel to a viewing platform where it emptied into the Niagara River. Don't miss out on this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to witness the beauty of Niagara Falls from a perspective few have ever experienced.
